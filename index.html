About Me

With over a decade of experience in visual storytelling, I craft narratives that resonate with audiences across cultures and platforms. My work explores the intersection of human emotion and cinematic technique, creating immersive experiences that linger long after the credits roll.

From intimate character studies to sweeping epics, my films have screened at international festivals and found homes on major OTT platforms. I believe in the power of cinema to challenge perspectives, evoke empathy, and illuminate the complexities of the human condition.

My approach combines meticulous visual composition with authentic performances, always in service of the story. Whether directing features, series, or short films, I bring the same dedication to craft and commitment to artistic excellence.
